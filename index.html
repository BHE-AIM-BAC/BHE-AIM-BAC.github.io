Brain-inspired Auditory Computation

Welcome to the BHE-AIM-BAC website!

Affiliated with the Key Laboratory of Brain Health Intelligent Evaluation and Intervention (Ministry of Education) , our lab operates at the cutting-edge intersection of medicine and engineering.

We are dedicated to advancing brain health research through the lens of brain-inspired auditory computation. We invite visitors from all disciplines to explore our research achievements and meet our team.

We look forward to collaborating with you to push the boundaries of neural science and intelligent technology.

Research

Grounded in interdisciplinary research, we conduct frontier exploration in the following core areas.

Brain-inspired Computer Audition

Developing biomimetic auditory models for robust speech processing in healthcare and emotion recognition.

Spiking Neural Networks

We leverage these advanced SNNs architectures to achieve ultra-low-power processing for long-term brain health monitoring and robust auditory perception.
